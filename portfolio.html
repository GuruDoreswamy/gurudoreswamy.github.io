AI-Driven Idea Generation & Evaluation

Problem

Engineering idea generation was slow, manual, and dependent on a small group of experts, limiting scalability and reuse of historical knowledge.

Approach

Designed an AI-assisted pipeline that transforms historical ideas into structured, evaluable concepts using NLP, generative models, and controlled prompting strategies.

System Design

Historical ideas → NLP-based clustering → AI-driven idea generation → rejection classification → human-in-the-loop evaluation → finalized ideas.

My Role

Led problem framing, model strategy definition, evaluation criteria design, and integration into engineering decision workflows.

Outcome

Generated 5–6 vetted ideas, reduced manual ideation effort, and enabled structured comparison and reuse of engineering concepts.
