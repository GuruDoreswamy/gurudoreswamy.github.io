AI-Driven Idea Generation & Evaluation

Business Objective

The objective was to realize USD 1.5M in cost savings across 15 product programs through structured idea generation. Approximately 250 contributors were expected to generate 8–10 ideas each, resulting in ~2,000 ideas at the enterprise level.

Problem Statement

The existing idea generation process was too slow and manual to meet the required scale. As participation increased, idea throughput did not keep pace and evaluation consistency varied across teams.

With scale, the effective approval rate dropped from ~30% to ~10%, significantly reducing the number of implementable ideas and putting the savings target at risk.

Approach

Designed an AI-assisted idea generation and evaluation pipeline that treats ideation as a structured decision process rather than ad-hoc brainstorming.

My Role

Led problem framing, defined success metrics aligned to business value, designed the end-to-end system architecture, and integrated the solution into enterprise-level engineering workflows.

Outcome

Improved ideation throughput, enabled structured filtering at scale, generated multiple vetted ideas for implementation, and strengthened traceability between ideas, decisions, and business outcomes.
